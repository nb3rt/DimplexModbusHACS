| 8 | Reserved | Return sensor short or open circuit |
| 9 | Reserved | Domestic hot water sensor short or open circuit |
| 10 | WPIO | Frost protection sensor short or open circuit |
| 11 | Reserved | 2nd heating circuit sensor short or open circuit |

| 13 | WQIF | Low pressure brine |
| 14 | Reserved | Motor protection primary |
| 15 | Sensor fault | Flow |
| 16 | Low pressure brine | Domestic hot water |
| 17 | Reserved | High pressure |
| 19 | Primary circuit fault | Hot gas thermostat |
| 20 | Defrost fault | Cooling operating limit |
| 21 | Low pressure brine fault | Reserved |
| 22 | Domestic hot water fault | Reserved |
